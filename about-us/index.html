About GhostGuard

We started in 2026 with one mission: to protect FiveM servers from cheaters while keeping performance at zero impact. What began as a small project has grown into a trusted anticheat solution.

Who we are

GhostGuard is a private, high-performance anticheat system built specifically for FiveM. We're a small dedicated team that understands the frustration of cheaters ruining the experience for legitimate players.

We're players first, developers second. Every feature we build is tested in real FiveM environments.

What we do

We provide real-time protection against godmode, noclip, speedhack, injections, and resource manipulation. Our system blocks thousands of cheat vectors while maintaining zero performance impact on your server.

10+ servers trust us to keep their communities clean and fair.
